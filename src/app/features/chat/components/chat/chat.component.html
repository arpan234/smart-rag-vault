RAG Architecture Explainer App

What is Retrieval-Augmented Generation?

Large Language Models (LLMs) are powerful but lack access to your private files. **RAG** solves this. Instead of fine-tuning, the app queries a database for relevant document facts and appends them to your prompt as context, ensuring answers are **100% accurate, private, and citation-backed**.

1

Chunk & Embed

Upload text or PDF files in the **Knowledge Vault**. We segment pages into overlapping paragraphs (chunks) and convert them into mathematical vectors (embeddings) stored in the browser's IndexedDB.

2

Vector Search

When you ask a question, we calculate the angle (**Cosine Similarity**) between your query vector and all stored document vectors. We retrieve the top-K chunks that represent the closest conceptual match.

3

LLM Generation

We decorate your prompt with the retrieved text paragraphs. The LLM processes this augmented context to synthesize a clean, factual answer backed by citations.
